Bingjing Tang

Postdoctoral Researcher
Department of Statistics, Stanford University

I work on Bayesian nonparametrics, generative modeling, coalescent models, Markov chain Monte Carlo, variational inference, and missing data. I have temporarily moved from Stanford Statistics to UCSF Biostatistics, and my previous Stanford email has not worked since December 2025.

I completed my PhD in Statistics at Purdue University under the supervision of Vinayak Rao. Before that, I received a master's degree from JHU AMS and an undergraduate degree in Management Information Systems, with a minor in English Literature, from Beijing Language and Culture University.
